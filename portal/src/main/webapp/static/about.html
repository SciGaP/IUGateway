About Science Gateways

A science gateway provides Web-based access to community-developed computing tools, scientific applications, scientific instruments, and data collections to support online science and simplify and expand the use of cyberinfrastructure. Scientific workflows power science gateways by providing the tools to manage complex applications and collections of applications on distributed computing resources.

The missions of the Science Gateway Group is to support the advancement of Web-based scientific research capabilities by developing open source, open community software; providing support for the integration of our software into operational science gateways; providing consulting support to scientific and scholarly communities for the development of new science gateways; and supporting core research and development for Web and distributed computing.

The Science Gateway and Workflow Group's primary focuses are the development of collaborative Web interfaces built from reusable components and network accessible services capable of managing complex scientific applications and collections of applications on computing clusters, grids, and computational clouds. The group's secondary focuses are providing expertise on open source and open community software processes, and providing consulting support for local, national, and international cyberinfrastructure projects
